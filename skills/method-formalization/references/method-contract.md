# Method contract

Give the contract an artifact ID, version, content hash, source idea ID/version, and `gate_ref: method_experiment`.

Use these sections:

1. **Scope and research claim**
2. **Problem setting**
3. **Assumptions and exclusions**
4. **Notation table** with symbol, meaning, domain/shape, unit, and first use
5. **Inputs, observations, outputs, and constraints**
6. **Objectives** with term-by-term rationale
7. **Atomic modules**
8. **Algorithm/pseudocode**
9. **Training, optimization, or estimation procedure**
10. **Inference/deployment procedure**
11. **Complexity, latency, and resource expectations**
12. **Safety, stability, or validity conditions**
13. **Structured predictions**
14. **Unresolved questions and implementation risks**
